## PE-flow-support

---

### 📌 Problem

- Game flow was frequently interrupted when the ball went out of bounds  
- Cold weather conditions reduced reaction speed and focus  
- Stable operation was required despite inexperienced staff  
- Beverages needed to be kept cold and delivered efficiently during the event  

---

### ⚙️ Approach

- Focused on maintaining continuous game flow through rapid ball retrieval  
- Tracked ball position and player movement to anticipate support needs  
- Minimized interruptions through quick, simple decision-making  
- Treated beverage handling as part of the operational flow, considering both cooling and delivery efficiency  

---

### 🛠️ Implementation

- Retrieved and returned balls immediately when they left the field  
- Moved proactively based on predicted ball trajectories and game flow  
- Reduced delays through concise, real-time decisions  
- Maintained focus despite cold environmental conditions  
- Transported and positioned a refrigerator on-site, considering space constraints  
- Supported powered cooling to preserve ice and beverages  
- Helped ensure smooth and timely distribution of drinks during the event  

---

### 💡 Insights

- Even minor roles directly impact overall system continuity and speed  
- Reaction speed comes from observation, anticipation, and adaptation  
- Systems often fail at their smallest operational points  
- Physical resources (e.g., refrigeration, cooling systems) are integral to operational flow
